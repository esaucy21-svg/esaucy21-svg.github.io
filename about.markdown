## Hello There, I'm Hadassah 👋

Welcom to my attachment journal - a personal space where i get to document my experiences in this lovely journey.

## What this journal is about

I decided to start this journal so that i can be able to track my progress as i navigate attacchment patterns, emotions and my personal growth too. Writing helps me process not only my emotions but my experiences too and it also helps me reflect on how far i've come.

## Why I write

I strongly believe that putting my feelings into words is one of the most powerful forms of healing. Thats why this is my safe space where i get to be honest, vulnerble and real most especially with myself.
