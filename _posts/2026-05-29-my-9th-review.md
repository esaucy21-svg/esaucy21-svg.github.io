## BANDIT WAREGAME
Over The Wire games is actually fun...
Starting at level 1 was quite challenging but i'm currently at level 9 and *let me tell you Maina😄*...the game is fun and very intresting.

I not only get to learn new commands but also learn how they are used like ways of handling spaces in filenames and using the find command to get to a file with specific properties, among other commands.

Not forgetting that there are a number of shortcuts to get a file rather than a normal long way, which makes things much easier, saving on time and also makes me look like a certain **guru😉**.
